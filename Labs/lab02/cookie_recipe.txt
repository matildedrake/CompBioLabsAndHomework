Nestle Tollhouse Recipe

1 buy a package of Nestle Tollhouse Chocolate Chip Cookies from Safeway
2 open the package
3 preheat the oven to 350 degrees
4 evenly space out cookies 2 inches apart on a baking tray
5 bake for 11 minutes
6 let cool for 5 minutes
7 enjoy with a glass of milk
